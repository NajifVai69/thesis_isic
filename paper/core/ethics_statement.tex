\section*{Ethics Statement}

This study uses only the publicly available ISIC-2019 dataset, which is
released for research use by the International Skin Imaging Collaboration.
No new human-subject data were collected, and all images are de-identified at
source. Patient metadata (age, sex, anatomical site) is used solely as model
input and is never re-identified. The work is intended as decision-support
research and is not a substitute for professional dermatological diagnosis.
